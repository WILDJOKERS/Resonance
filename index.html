About

DJ Resonance is an emerging engineer and electronic musician. Resonance synthesizes live guitars and punchy hip hop beats to create an innovative musical fusion. Employing modern audio mixing equipment, Resonance seamlessly blends  analog and digital sounds.

Performer

Resonance has performed internationally, including an Israel debut at Galina in Tel Aviv with DJ Suiss and Houston Mack opening for DJ Shlomi Levi. Locally, Resonance spins in the Manhattan area, having opened for acclaimed DJs including Libra9 and DJ Price at venues such as Motivo and EVR. Resonance inserts contemporary lyrical themes into modern electronic builds to breathe an emotional crescendo into every audience member.

Producer

Apart from performing and mixing his personal repertoire, Resonance produces and engineers up-and-coming musical artists including electro-pop singer M.C. Pollock, rapper Dok Apokalypse of ATS crew, and electronic musician Anti-Citizen One. A certified gear head, Resonance's mission is to explore the most crisp, quality sounds that incite audience excitement.
